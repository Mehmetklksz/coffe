Your Coffee Questions Answered

Find quick answers to common questions about our café, menu, and services.

Are you pet-friendly?

Yes! We love pets. Your furry friends are always welcome inside our cafe, and we even have water bowls ready for them.

Do you offer gluten-free options?

We certainly do. We offer a variety of gluten-free pastries and milk alternatives to make sure everyone enjoys their time.

What vegan milk options do you have?

We provide oat milk, almond milk, and soy milk as non-dairy alternatives for all our espresso-based drinks.

Can I buy coffee beans for home brewing?

Absolutely! All our single-origin beans and signature blends are available in 250g bags, freshly roasted and ground to your preference.

Still have questions?

Our friendly staff is always happy to help. Reach out to us directly for any inquiries not covered here.
